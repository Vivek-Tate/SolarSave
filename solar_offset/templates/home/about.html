About Solar Save

Our mission at SolarSave is to offer you and your household the means to offset your carbon footprint by donating towards projects that aim to install solar panels worldwide.

Our team is working continuously on providing you with the best information and experience so that can make informed decisions for donating your money in order to offset your carbon footprint.

If you have any questions or other queries, feel free to reach out by Contacting us.
