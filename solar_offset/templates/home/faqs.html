FAQ

Find the answers for the most frequently asked questions below

Why Should I Donate my Money?

By no means you have to feel obligated to donate to SolarSave. We are simply offering a means to let you do your part in combatting climate change by offestting your households carbon footprint.

I want to Donate to a Country that's not on Your List.

We are always working on expanding SolarSave. If you have any ideas or suggestions, please feel free to Contact us.

Are your Payments secure?

We are doing our utmost to ensuring that any donations are transferred securely. Currently our payments are processed through PayPal which is a secure and reliable payment provider, even when you don't have an account with them.

Where Does my Money go?

Your donations are directly transferred to the organizations that you choose. While we provide you with the tools to donate, no part of your donation is ever directly held by us.

What if I want a refund?

While we offer the means for you to make donations, we are never directly handling the money that you choose to donate. As a result, we do not have any power to refund the money that you donated. If you wish try and refund your donation, please contact the organization that you donated to, Paypal's customer support, or your bank.
